>
> **Discussion:** The discussion is largely positive, with a consensus that SAM 3 represents a "GPT moment" for computer vision, particularly due to its open-vocabulary capabilities. However, the conversation quickly pivots from the model itself to the ecosystem and practical applications.

**Key Insights & Consensus:**
*   **Distillation is the Killer App:** The most significant use case highlighted (by Roboflow) is not direct usage, but using SAM 3 as a "teacher" model to distill knowledge into smaller, faster, real-time models. This solves the latency issue for production deployment.

**Disagreements & Concerns:**
*   **Real-Time Feasibility:** There is debate about whether SAM 3 can be used for real-time video processing directly. Overall, the community views SAM 3 as a foundational shift that automates the tedious process of data labeling and segmentation, enabling rapid prototyping of specialized vision models.
