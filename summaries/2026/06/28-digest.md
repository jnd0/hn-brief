The sync between a commercial, marketing‑driven AI launch and a grassroots, code‑based model release indicates that the industry’s reliance on “proprietary advantage” is wearing thin. The real irony is that the most disruptive AI progress seems to be brewed in the very environments that have historically avoided “big‑data” headaches.
