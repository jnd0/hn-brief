The divergence in global policy highlights a critical tension: Can democracy keep pace with the velocity of technological change, or will it become a relic in the race to dominate AI?

Governments are scrambling to update their defenses, but the pace of innovation far outstrips bureaucratic inertia. The result is a patchwork of ad hoc solutions, leaving critical infrastructure vulnerable to attack.
