**Score:** 894 | **Comments:** 276 | **ID:** 48542480

Others point out that Iroh’s design reuses IETF standards such as QUIC and TLS, which makes it interoperable with existing tools, and that its open‑source core can be embedded directly into applications without requiring user accounts.
