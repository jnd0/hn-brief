The move sends a clear signal that the company values community-driven innovation more than its own legacy. This is a strategic play, especially considering the broader industry trend of monopolizing aspects of hardware without sharing the underlying tech. The README’s caution—properly worded—serves as a reminder that while the intent is laudable, the practical limitations of relying on firmware software still exist.
