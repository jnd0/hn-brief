Technical threads examined the vulnerability of Signal’s desktop client, the role of disk encryption (BitLocker/FileVault), and whether a compromised laptop could expose sources despite the iPhone’s protection. Comparisons were drawn to Android’s lack of similar power‑button shortcuts and to hardware‑based solutions like GrapheneOS or Samsung’s Secure Folder, illustrating divergent views on platform resilience. Overall, the community expressed both admiration for Lockdown Mode’s effectiveness and concern over its usability trade‑offs, sparking a lively exchange of practical tips and deeper security analysis.
