By truncating the series at the fourth order, the authors claim the element‑wise error is comparable to the precision of Float16 arithmetic. >
> **Discussion:** Commenters quickly challenged the notion that true attention can be made sub‑quadratic, citing lower‑bound papers that argue any exact retrieval of all token‑pair information must remain O(n²). Some participants pointed out practical issues—whether the constant‑cost claim applies to training, inference, or both, and whether GPUs handle the extra polynomial kernels efficiently.
