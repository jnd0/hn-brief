The original article is identified as a republication of a 2008 press release.
*   **The Actual Artifact:** A PhD researcher in Babylonian Astronomy (user willismonroe) clarifies that the tablet (K.8538) is a well-known object that has been translated. It is considered a product of scholarly astronomical/astrological thinking from the Neo-Assyrian period, not a direct observational record of an impact. The popular "star map" depiction is often a poorly made replica, and the original artifact is not an impact record.

In summary, the discussion concludes that the article promotes a discredited and sensationalist theory.
