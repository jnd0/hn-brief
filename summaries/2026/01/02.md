The attack's four-step process—connecting, exfiltrating the link key, and then impersonating the headphones to attack the phone—was highlighted as particularly concerning. The general sentiment was that this is a significant threat, with some speculating that state actors would be both happy and unhappy depending on whether they knew about the exploit beforehand. A few users also noted that high-profile figures like Kamala Harris have publicly stated they avoid wireless headphones for security reasons.

The conversation also veered into the familiar debate about the removal of the 3.5mm headphone jack.
