Users also debated the installation method. While burying cables directly in concrete is risky, the author had used PVC trunking (conduit). Commenters noted that this was a crucial saving grace, as it would allow for the disintegrated cable to be pulled out and a new one threaded through, despite the challenges of the long run with many bends. The consensus was that standard, more resilient cables would have been a better choice, and that planning for future replacement with accessible conduit is essential.
