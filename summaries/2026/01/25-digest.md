Whether it's reclaiming digital sovereignty from U.S. tech, finding pragmatic ways to manage engineering timelines, or simply debugging a system that's failed after 66 days, the underlying theme is a desire for systems we can understand, trust, and, when necessary, bend to our will. The old models—of closed platforms, opaque governance, and monolithic infrastructure—are showing their cracks.
