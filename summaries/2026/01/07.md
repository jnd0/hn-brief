Zig is mentioned as a potential middle ground due to its C compatibility, but it's deemed too new for a project like SQLite.

*   **Specific Technical Criticisms:** A recurring point of contention is Rust's handling of out-of-memory (OOM) errors, a concern shared by both the SQLite author and Linux kernel developers.
