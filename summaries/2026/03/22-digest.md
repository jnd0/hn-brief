The debate spills into meta‑level concerns about the addictive nature of AI interfaces, drawing parallels to social‑media consumption and the way dopamine‑driven feedback can overwrite genuine curiosity. Several commenters propose concrete safeguards – manual “System 3” thinking guides, custom scripts that force step‑by‑step verification, and even a personal “thinking‑tool” project aimed at rebuilding pedantic clarity – signaling a desire to harness AI without surrendering agency.
