One seasoned dev pointed out that the biggest win he’s seen is in rapid research and debugging, not in designing novel features that require deep architectural insight. The discussion spirals into a debate about whether senior engineers are actually more productive when they lean on AI, or whether they are simply outsourcing the grunt work to an algorithm that hallucinates assumptions.
